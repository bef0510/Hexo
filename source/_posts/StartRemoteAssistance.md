# 開啟遠端桌面
1. 開啟 `Server Manager`
![Architecture](1.png)

2. 點選 `Local Server` → 點選 `Remote Desktop`
![Architecture](2.png)

3. 選擇 `Remote` Tab → 選擇 `Allo remote connections to this computer`
![Architecture](3.png)
